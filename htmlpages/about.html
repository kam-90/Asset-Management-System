About AidVanTech

The world of support management systems such as customer relationship management systems and IT Management Systems are incredibly focus on optimizing customer interactions above all else.

Our group has made the observation that this has created a potential market to make support management systems for organizations whose support is primarily focused on proper asset management over optimized customer relations management.

AVT is not about making a support management system for a niche industry such as the HVAC industry, instead our aim is to make a system that empowers these companies to make their very own asset management system
